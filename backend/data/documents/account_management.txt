Account Management Guide

Managing Your Account

Creating an Account:
- Click "Sign Up" on the homepage
- Enter your email address and create a password
- Verify your email address through the confirmation link
- Complete your profile information
- Start shopping!

Updating Profile Information:
1. Log into your account
2. Click on "Account Settings" in the dropdown menu
3. Update your personal information (name, email, phone)
4. Click "Save Changes" to update

Managing Addresses:
- You can save multiple shipping and billing addresses
- Set a default address for faster checkout
- Edit or delete addresses at any time
- Address book is located in "Account Settings"

Changing Your Email:
1. Go to "Account Settings"
2. Click "Change Email"
3. Enter your new email address
4. Verify the new email through confirmation link
5. Your old email will no longer work for login

Changing Your Password:
1. Go to "Account Settings"
2. Click "Change Password"
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password (min 8 characters)
5. Confirm your new password
6. Click "Update Password"

Email Preferences:
- Manage your email subscriptions in "Communication Preferences"
- Choose to receive promotional emails, order updates, or newsletters
- Update preferences at any time
- Unsubscribe links are in every marketing email

Closing Your Account:
- Contact customer support to request account closure
- All personal data will be deleted within 30 days
- Order history will be retained for legal compliance
- This action cannot be undone

For account issues, contact support@example.com or call 1-800-SUPPORT.
