Shipping Information

Shipping Methods and Delivery Times

We offer multiple shipping options to meet your needs:

Standard Shipping (5-7 business days):
- Free on orders over $50
- $5.99 for orders under $50
- Available to all US addresses

Express Shipping (2-3 business days):
- $12.99 flat rate
- Available to all US addresses
- Orders must be placed before 2 PM EST for same-day processing

Overnight Shipping (1 business day):
- $24.99 flat rate
- Available to most US addresses
- Orders must be placed before 12 PM EST for next-day delivery
- Not available for PO boxes or APO/FPO addresses

International Shipping:
- Available to select countries
- Delivery time varies by destination (7-21 business days)
- Customs fees and duties may apply
- Contact support for rates and availability

Order Tracking:
- You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships
- Track your package at any time through your account dashboard
- Tracking updates may take 24-48 hours to appear after shipment

Shipping Restrictions:
- We do not ship to PO boxes for express or overnight delivery
- Some items may have shipping restrictions based on size or weight
- Additional charges may apply for oversized items

For shipping questions, contact us at shipping@example.com or call 1-800-SHIPPING.
