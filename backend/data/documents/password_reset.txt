How to Reset Your Password

If you've forgotten your password, you can easily reset it by following these steps:

1. Go to the login page
2. Click on "Forgot Password?" link below the login form
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Check your email for a password reset link (it may take up to 5 minutes to arrive)
5. Click the reset link in the email
6. Enter your new password (must be at least 8 characters with a mix of letters and numbers)
7. Confirm your new password
8. Click "Reset Password" to complete the process

If you don't receive the reset email within 10 minutes, please check your spam folder. If you still have issues, contact our support team at support@example.com.

For security reasons, password reset links expire after 24 hours.
