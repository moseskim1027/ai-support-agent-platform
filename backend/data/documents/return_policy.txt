Return and Refund Policy

Our Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

Return Eligibility:
- Items must be unused and in original packaging
- Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
- Items on sale or clearance can be returned for store credit only
- Custom or personalized items cannot be returned

How to Initiate a Return:
1. Log into your account and go to "Order History"
2. Select the order you wish to return
3. Click "Request Return" and select the items
4. Choose your refund method (original payment method or store credit)
5. Print the prepaid return shipping label
6. Pack the items securely in original packaging
7. Drop off at any authorized shipping location

Refund Processing:
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return
- Original shipping costs are non-refundable
- Store credit refunds are issued immediately upon receipt
- You'll receive an email confirmation once your refund is processed

For questions about returns, contact our support team at returns@example.com or call 1-800-RETURNS.
