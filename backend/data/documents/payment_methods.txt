Payment Methods and Billing

Accepted Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

Credit and Debit Cards:
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover
- All major debit cards with Visa/Mastercard logo

Digital Wallets:
- PayPal
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- Shop Pay

Other Payment Options:
- Gift cards and store credit
- Buy Now, Pay Later services (Afterpay, Klarna)
- Bank transfers for orders over $1000

Payment Security:
- All transactions are encrypted with SSL technology
- We never store your complete credit card information
- PCI-DSS compliant payment processing
- 3D Secure authentication for added security

Billing Information:
- Your billing address must match your payment method
- We verify billing information for fraud prevention
- International orders may require additional verification

Gift Cards:
- Available in denominations from $25 to $500
- Never expire and have no fees
- Can be used online or in store
- Check balance at any time in your account

Store Credit:
- Issued for returns or as promotional credits
- Applied automatically at checkout
- Can be combined with other payment methods
- View your store credit balance in "Account Settings"

Payment Issues:
If your payment is declined:
1. Verify your billing information is correct
2. Check with your bank for any restrictions
3. Ensure you have sufficient funds
4. Try a different payment method
5. Contact our support team for assistance

Refunds and Chargebacks:
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- Allow 5-7 business days for refunds to appear
- Chargebacks may result in account suspension
- Contact us before filing a chargeback

For payment questions, email billing@example.com or call 1-800-BILLING.
