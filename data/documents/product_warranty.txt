Product Warranty Information

Standard Warranty:
All products come with a 1-year limited warranty against manufacturing defects.

What's Covered:
- Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship
- Malfunctions under normal use conditions
- Parts and labor for covered repairs
- Replacement if repair is not possible

What's NOT Covered:
- Normal wear and tear
- Accidental damage or misuse
- Damage from unauthorized repairs
- Cosmetic damage that doesn't affect functionality
- Products with removed or altered serial numbers
- Consumable parts (batteries, filters, etc.)

Warranty Period:
- Standard products: 1 year from purchase date
- Premium products: 2 years from purchase date
- Refurbished products: 90 days from purchase date

Filing a Warranty Claim:
1. Contact our support team with your order number
2. Describe the issue and provide photos if possible
3. We'll diagnose the problem remotely when possible
4. If a warranty claim is approved, we'll provide a prepaid shipping label
5. Send the product to our repair center
6. We'll repair or replace within 10-15 business days

Extended Warranty:
- Purchase extended coverage for an additional 1-2 years
- Must be bought within 30 days of original purchase
- Includes accidental damage protection
- Faster replacement times (3-5 business days)

Service Centers:
- Use our service center locator to find authorized repair locations
- Some repairs can be done locally without shipping
- Always use authorized service providers to maintain warranty coverage

Product Registration:
- Register your product online to activate warranty
- Receive important product updates and recalls
- Speeds up warranty claim processing

Proof of Purchase:
- Keep your receipt or order confirmation
- Required for all warranty claims
- We can look up your order if you have an account
