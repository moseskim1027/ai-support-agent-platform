Return Policy

Our company offers a 30-day return policy for most products. Here are the details:

Return Window:
- You have 30 days from the date of purchase to return an item
- The product must be unused and in its original packaging
- All accessories, manuals, and parts must be included

Return Process:
1. Log into your account and go to Order History
2. Select the order containing the item you want to return
3. Click "Request Return" and select the reason
4. Print the prepaid return label we email you
5. Package the item securely and attach the label
6. Drop off at any authorized shipping location

Refund Timeline:
- Once we receive your return, allow 5-7 business days for inspection
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- It may take 3-5 additional business days for the refund to appear

Non-Returnable Items:
- Opened software or digital products
- Personalized or custom-made items
- Health and personal care items
- Gift cards
- Final sale items

Exchanges:
- We don't offer direct exchanges
- Please return the item for a refund and place a new order
- If the item is defective, we'll cover return shipping
