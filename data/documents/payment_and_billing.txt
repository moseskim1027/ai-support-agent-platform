Payment and Billing Information

Accepted Payment Methods:
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- PayPal and PayPal Credit
- Apple Pay and Google Pay
- Gift cards and store credit
- Affirm (buy now, pay later)

Payment Security:
- All transactions are encrypted with SSL/TLS
- We use PCI-DSS compliant payment processors
- We never store your full credit card number
- Your payment information is tokenized for security

Pricing:
- All prices are in USD unless otherwise stated
- Prices are subject to change without notice
- Sale prices are valid for the duration specified
- Taxes are calculated at checkout based on shipping address

Sales Tax:
- Collected based on your shipping address
- Rates vary by state and local jurisdiction
- Tax-exempt organizations must provide documentation

Promotional Codes:
- Enter codes at checkout in the "Promo Code" field
- One code per order unless otherwise stated
- Cannot be combined with other offers typically
- Codes have expiration dates
- Some exclusions may apply

Order Confirmation:
- You'll receive an email confirmation immediately after purchase
- Check your spam folder if you don't see it
- Contact us within 24 hours if you notice any errors

Billing Statement:
- Charges appear as "EXAMPLE COMPANY" on your statement
- Order number is included in the transaction description
- If you don't recognize a charge, contact us immediately

Failed Payments:
- We'll notify you if your payment doesn't go through
- Common reasons: insufficient funds, expired card, incorrect information
- Update your payment method and try again
- Orders are held for 48 hours before being cancelled

Refunds and Credits:
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- Allow 5-10 business days for refunds to appear
- Store credit is applied immediately to your account
- Gift card refunds may be issued as store credit

Invoices:
- Available in your account under Order History
- Can be downloaded as PDF
- Business customers can request detailed invoices with tax ID

Subscription Billing:
- Recurring charges for subscription services
- Billed automatically on renewal date
- Cancel anytime before the next billing cycle
- Email reminders sent 7 days before renewal

Disputes and Chargebacks:
- Contact us first before disputing a charge
- Most issues can be resolved quickly
- Chargebacks may result in account suspension
- We work with you to resolve billing concerns
