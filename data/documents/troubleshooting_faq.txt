Frequently Asked Questions & Troubleshooting

Account Issues:

Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions in the reset email we send you.

Q: I'm not receiving your emails. What should I do?
A: Check your spam/junk folder, add us to your safe senders list, and verify your email address in account settings.

Q: How do I change my email address?
A: Log into your account, go to Settings > Profile, update your email, and verify the new address.

Ordering Issues:

Q: Can I cancel my order?
A: Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order may have already been shipped. Contact us immediately.

Q: How do I track my package?
A: Check your shipping confirmation email for the tracking number, or view it in your account under Order History.

Q: My package is delayed. What should I do?
A: Check the tracking information first. If it's been more than 2 days past the expected delivery date, contact our support team.

Q: I received the wrong item. What now?
A: Contact us immediately with photos of the item you received. We'll send you the correct item and provide a prepaid return label.

Product Issues:

Q: My product isn't working. Is it defective?
A: First, check the troubleshooting section in the product manual. If that doesn't help, contact our technical support team.

Q: How do I clean/maintain my product?
A: Refer to the care instructions in your product manual. Use only recommended cleaning products.

Q: Where can I find replacement parts?
A: Visit our Parts & Accessories section or contact support with your model number.

Payment Issues:

Q: Why was my payment declined?
A: Common reasons include insufficient funds, expired card, incorrect billing address, or bank security holds. Contact your bank if the issue persists.

Q: When will I see my refund?
A: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. Allow 3-5 additional days for it to appear in your account.

Q: Can I use multiple payment methods for one order?
A: Currently, we only accept one payment method per order, but you can combine it with gift cards.

Technical Issues:

Q: The website isn't working properly. What should I do?
A: Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, or use a different browser. Contact us if the problem persists.

Q: I can't upload my profile photo.
A: Ensure the image is in JPG or PNG format and under 5MB. Try a different browser if the issue continues.

Q: The mobile app keeps crashing.
A: Update to the latest version, restart your device, and reinstall the app if necessary.

Shipping Issues:

Q: Can I change my shipping address after ordering?
A: If the order hasn't shipped yet, contact us immediately. We can't change addresses for orders already in transit.

Q: What if my package is stolen?
A: File a police report and contact us. We'll work with the carrier to investigate and help resolve the issue.

Q: Do you ship to P.O. boxes?
A: Standard shipping is available to P.O. boxes, but expedited shipping is not.
