Shipping Information

We offer multiple shipping options to meet your needs:

Standard Shipping:
- Cost: FREE on orders over $50, otherwise $5.99
- Delivery time: 5-7 business days
- Available for all addresses in the continental US
- No signature required

Express Shipping:
- Cost: $14.99
- Delivery time: 2-3 business days  
- Available for most US addresses
- Tracking included

Overnight Shipping:
- Cost: $29.99
- Delivery time: Next business day
- Order by 2 PM EST for same-day processing
- Signature required upon delivery

International Shipping:
- Available to select countries
- Costs vary by destination (calculated at checkout)
- Delivery time: 10-20 business days
- Customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient

Order Processing:
- Orders placed before 2 PM EST ship the same business day
- Orders placed after 2 PM EST ship the next business day
- We don't ship on weekends or holidays
- You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships

Tracking Your Order:
- Check your email for the tracking number
- Visit our Order Status page and enter your order number
- Sign into your account to view all order details

Package Protection:
- All packages are insured up to $100
- For higher-value orders, additional insurance is recommended
- Contact us within 48 hours if your package arrives damaged
