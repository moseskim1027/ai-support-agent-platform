Account Management Guide

Creating an Account:
- Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
- Enter your email address and create a password
- Verify your email by clicking the link we send you
- Complete your profile with shipping and billing information

Password Reset:
- Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
- Enter your email address
- Check your email for a reset link (valid for 24 hours)
- Click the link and enter your new password
- Your new password must be at least 8 characters long

Updating Account Information:
- Log into your account
- Go to Settings > Account > Profile
- Update your personal information, email, or phone number
- Click "Save Changes" to apply updates

Managing Addresses:
- Navigate to Settings > Account > Addresses
- Add new shipping or billing addresses
- Set a default address for faster checkout
- Edit or delete existing addresses

Payment Methods:
- Go to Settings > Account > Payment Methods
- Add credit/debit cards or PayPal accounts
- We securely store payment information for faster checkout
- Your full card number is never displayed for security

Email Preferences:
- Manage your email subscriptions in Settings > Notifications
- Choose which emails you want to receive:
  * Order confirmations and shipping updates
  * Promotional offers and discounts
  * Product recommendations
  * Newsletter and blog updates

Privacy Settings:
- Control who can see your profile information
- Manage data sharing preferences
- Download your personal data
- Request account deletion

Two-Factor Authentication:
- Enable 2FA for added security
- Use SMS or authenticator app
- Required for high-value purchases

Deleting Your Account:
- Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
- Review the consequences of deletion
- Confirm your decision
- Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
